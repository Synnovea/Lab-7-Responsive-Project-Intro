Magical Photos By Alexandria's Lens

Honestly, looking at tese amazing photos by Alexandria that she takes of her family are truly beautiful and amazing. Once I knew we would be doing an assignment on creative art, I just knew I had to bring these photos to other people's attention if they had not seen them yet. I hope you are transported to a time where you believed in magic like I have after viewing these photos. Hoping that one day I can achieve something as beautiful as this one day.

Fun Facts about this Family

Alexandria, who is the creator of these photos, is a self-taught photographer. (Crazy great for her!!!) She loves creating magical photos with her husband and her three lovely children. A you can see from the images, she lovs to take classic fairytales that she might have told her children, and that we have probably heard of growing up, and incorporated them in her photos. Who wouldn't want to be in a real-life fairytale?
